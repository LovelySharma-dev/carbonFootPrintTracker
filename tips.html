Simple Tips to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

  • Use public transport or cycle whenever possible
  • Reduce single-use plastics and recycle
  • Switch to energy-efficient bulbs and appliances
  • Turn off lights and electronics when not in use
  • Eat more local and plant-based foods
  • Take shorter showers to save water
  • Plant trees and support green projects
